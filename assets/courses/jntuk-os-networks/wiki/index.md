# OS & Networks — Wiki Index

## Overview
Operating Systems & Computer Networks is part of the JNTUK R20 B.Tech CSE curriculum. This course provides a comprehensive foundation aligned to the official JNTUK syllabus with additional placement-preparation content for campus drives at TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Cognizant, Accenture, and product-based companies.

## Key Concepts

### Course Objectives
This course develops the theoretical understanding and practical skills required to excel in JNTUK university examinations (2 × 30-mark mid-term tests + 70-mark end-semester exam) and campus placement technical assessments.

### JNTUK Examination Pattern
- **Mid Semester Exam (MSE)**: 30 marks × 2 = 60 marks (best of 2 attempts, 3 attempts offered)
- **End Semester Exam (ESE)**: 70 marks (5 questions from 5 units, each with internal choice)
- **Lab**: 25 marks internal + 50 marks end-semester practical exam
- **Total**: 100 marks theory + 75 marks lab (where applicable)

### Units Overview
The JNTUK R20 syllabus divides each subject into 5 units. Each unit is covered in 12-15 lecture hours. The course wiki pages cover all 5 units in depth with examples, previous exam questions, and placement-oriented extensions.
